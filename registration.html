Registration

Attendance will be limited to 150 participants.

Priority will be given to speakers and poster presenters.

Please submit an abstract for a contributed talk or a poster through EasyChair here.
The deadline for abstract and poster submissions is March 1st, 2024 at 11:59 PM CET.

Contributed talks will be given in 20 min slots (15 min presentation + 5 min discussion).

Notification of acceptance will be given by March 15th, 2024 at the latest

Registration

All participants are required to register. Registration is free of charge.

Coffee breaks will be offered on site. A social event which will take place on the evening of Thursday May 30th 2024. More details will follow.
