Registration

The registration fee is:

Researchers, Professors: 150 euros

Master students: free


For any question please write an email to liph . unipd @ gmail.com

Fee waivers

To apply for the fee waiver, please send your abstract via the Easy Chair link in the Abstract submission section, and send an email with your CV and two lines of motivations to liph.unipd@gmail.com with subject "SMEEB 2024 Fee Waiver Request".
